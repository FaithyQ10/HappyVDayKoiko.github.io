♡ Letter for You ♡

Will you be my Valentine?

I'm so happy to be with my forever best friend! I love you like doctor who loves its christmas episodes and as deeply as Riku and Schwi. I'm so grateful to have met you on Sky by mere chance. However it is only one of many good days when it comes to you. You brighten my life with your kindness. My one true home is by your side, the warmth of your soft embrace. I know you don't like to hear me say it, but thank you for being here. I want to spend my life with you my sweet girl, if you will have me. I will support you, defend you, and love you no matter what comes our way. I can't wait to meet my favourite person.
